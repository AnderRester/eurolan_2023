About

The EUROLAN series of Summer Schools, established in 1993, provides comprehensive coverage of topics that are particularly relevant to the fields of computational linguistics and natural language processing. The goal of the 16th EUROLAN2023 School is to bring together specialists, professors, and students in relevant disciplines as linguistics, natural language processing, and information technology to discuss architectures, resources, evaluation and implications for Generative AI.
